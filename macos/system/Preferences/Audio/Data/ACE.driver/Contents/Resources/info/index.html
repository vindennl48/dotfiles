Audio Capture Engine (ACE)

What It Is

Rogue Amoeba's Audio Capture Engine (ACE) powers audio capture for many applications, including Rogue Amoeba's tools.

Installing

To install ACE, just follow the prompts. Enter your password when requested, and you should be all set.

The ACE components will be placed in the /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/HAL/ folder.

Uninstalling

To uninstall ACE, choose the “Uninstall” option within an application that uses ACE.
